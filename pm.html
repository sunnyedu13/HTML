The Prime Minister of India

Introduction to the prime Minister

The prime MInister of India is the head of the goverment, responsible for overseeing the functioning of the executive branch and making crucial decisions for country's Development. The Prime Minister plays a pivotal role in the policy-making process and represents India on a global platform.

Roles and Responsibilities

The Prime Minister holds Various significant Responsibilities, inculding:

              

  • Leading the Executive:
    • The Prime Minister leads the cabinet of ministers, formulating national policies and ensuring their implementation.

  • Foeign Affairs:
    • The Prime Minister represents India in internationals forums and negotiations.

  • Legislative Functions:
    • As a member of parliament, the prime minister plays an active role in passing laws.
